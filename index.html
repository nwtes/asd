First Treatment Tips

Quick, practical guidance you can start today. Explore more during your session.

Managing Anxiety

  • Box breathing: 4-4-4-4 count for 2 minutes.
  • Limit caffeine and news before noon.
  • Grounding: name 5 things you see.

Coping with Depression

  • Set one small, doable goal daily.
  • Morning light: 10 minutes by a window.
  • Reach out to a trusted friend.
Learn More

Easing Stress

  • 90-second pause before reacting.
  • Schedule a 10-minute walk break.
  • Single-task: silence notifications.

Better Sleep

  • Keep a consistent bedtime.
  • Dim screens 1 hour before bed.
  • Keep the bedroom cool and dark.
